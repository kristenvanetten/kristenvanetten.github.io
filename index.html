I have a background working in multiple types of media and am passionate about all kinds of stories. I believe that good storytellers are able to work in different formats to best fit their content. I've spent my career honing my skills to do this, developing my expertise with text, visuals, and interactive media.

About Me

I have a degree in Television-Radio-Film from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University, with a minor in Information Management and Technology. Expertise in these dual programs has allowed me to combine my creative side with my technical abilities. I've always been strong in both areas, and was drawn to digital content because it allowed me to use both skill sets throughout my career.

I've worked as a producer, writer, editor, and instructional designer and have over 15 years of experience in digital content development and publishing. I currently coordinate with cross-functional teams to create unique, engaging, and user-friendly online content.

Skills and Experience
