Photography

I love to travel and take a lot of photos of any new place I visit. I've seen many locations with scenery very different from home that is quite beautiful, and I like to capture that in photos. I focus on photographing landscapes with water in them because I think it creates a dramatic look. I also like taking photos of sunsets because of the impact of the changing light and color over different backgrounds.

Below are some examples of pictures I have taken.
