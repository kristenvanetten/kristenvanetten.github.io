Kristen Van Etten

As an instructional designer with a background working in multiple types of media, I'm passionate about all kinds of stories. I believe that good storytellers are able to work in different formats to best fit their content and to always tell their stories. I've spent my career honing my skills to do this, developing my expertise with text, visuals, and interactive media.

Read more about me below.

About Me

I have a degree in Television-Radio-Film from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University, with a minor in Information Management and Technology. Expertise in these dual programs has allowed me to combine my creative side with my technical abilities. I have always been strong in both areas, and was drawn to digital content because it allowed me to use both skill sets throughout my career.

I've worked as a producer, writer, editor, and instructional designer and have over 14 years of experience in digital content development and publishing. I currently coordinate with cross-functional teams to create unique, engaging, and user-friendly online content.
